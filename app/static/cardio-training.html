Cardio Training Programs

Endurance Booster

This program is designed to improve your cardiovascular endurance by mixing steady-state cardio with interval training.

  • 4 days per week
  • Steady-state and HIIT sessions
  • Focus on stamina and heart health

HIIT Cardio Blast

This program uses high-intensity interval training to maximize calorie burn and improve cardiovascular performance.

  • 3 days per week
  • Short bursts of intense exercise
  • Focus on fat loss and speed

Steady-State Cardio

Perfect for beginners, this program focuses on consistent, moderate-intensity cardio sessions to build endurance.

  • 5 days per week
  • Long-duration, moderate-intensity workouts
  • Focus on gradual stamina improvement

Interval Training Challenge

This challenging program alternates between high-speed sprints and recovery periods to push your cardiovascular limits.

  • 4 days per week
  • Alternating sprint and recovery intervals
  • Designed for maximum cardiovascular improvement

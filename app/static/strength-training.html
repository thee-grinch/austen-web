Muscle Training Programs

Beginner Muscle Builder

This program is designed for beginners to build a strong foundation. It focuses on proper form, light weights, and full-body exercises.

  • 3 days per week
  • Basic compound movements
  • Progressive overload technique

Intermediate Strength Routine

For those who have mastered the basics, this program increases intensity with advanced exercises and higher weights to build muscle mass and strength.

  • 4 days per week
  • Advanced compound and isolation exercises
  • Focus on hypertrophy and strength gains

Power Lifting Challenge

Targeted at advanced lifters, this program emphasizes heavy lifts and explosive movements to maximize power and strength.

  • 5 days per week
  • Heavy squat, bench, and deadlift sessions
  • Technique-focused training cycles

HIIT & Strength Fusion

This innovative program combines high-intensity interval training with strength exercises, ideal for boosting endurance and muscle definition simultaneously.

  • 3-4 days per week
  • Short, intense workouts
  • Mix of cardio and resistance training
